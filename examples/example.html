2. Waking up from dream
We see Claire, wearing her signature plain red dress laying on her bed. The early morning sun projects onto her creating a lazy scene. See stays laying down for a second...
...then her alarm sets off, she shoots awake. Annoyed, she slowly raises her hand and slams the clock off the table. It hits the ground with a dry thud. She lets out a sigh of defeat, begrudgingly stands up, brushes off dust from her red dress and is good to go. Claire puts her shoes on, brushes her teeth and hair (using different utensils).
